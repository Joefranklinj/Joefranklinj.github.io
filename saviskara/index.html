The Communication Club of the college is a social and educational group for those interested in improving English communication.
The Tamil club is a fun loving club. The club serves as an outlet for the students to exhibit talent and enjoy.

fine arts club

The primary objectives of the Fine Arts Club are to unite the KPRIET student body in the appreciation of the disciplines of computer graphic design, English, film, music, dance, theatre and visual art. The Fine Arts Club strives to provide opportunities for active awareness of the fine arts for students and community, and to build a working relationship for creative outreach among the college and the local community.

Photography & Cinematography Club

KPRIET Photography and Cinematography Club was setup to encourage students already in the hobby and also others interested to learn about photography. The club conducts workshops on photography and photo competitions.
